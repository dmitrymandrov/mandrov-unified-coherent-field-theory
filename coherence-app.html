MU-CFT: Coherent Quantum Branch Selector

Coherence Functional: $C(B_i) = P(B_i) \cdot e^{-\Delta S(B_i)} \cdot E(B_i)$

Subjective Probability: $P_{\text{subjective}}(B_i) = \frac{C(B_i)}{\sum_j C(B_j)$

$P(Bi)$: probability of the branch (between 0 and 1)

$ΔS(Bi)$: entropy increase for the branch

$E(Bi)$: energy coherence or stability (higher is better)
