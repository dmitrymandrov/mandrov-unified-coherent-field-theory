About the Theory

MU-CFT (Mandrov Unified Coherent Field Theory) is a theoretical framework uniting quantum physics, gravity, information, and observer dynamics into a coherent whole. Reality, in this approach, is not an objective absolute but emerges through structured subjective coherence.

This theory explores how conscious observers influence physical law through coherence — a principle that selects stable, consistent, and meaningful branches of possibility.

"MU-CFT is not a theory of everything — it is a mirror of how everything holds together."

Core Concepts

  • Subjective Coherence Functional: reweights quantum branches to ensure a consistent observer experience.
  • Coherence Field: the scalar field $\mathcal{C}(x^\mu)$ encoding coherence density and subjective dynamics.
  • Space of Potentials $\Omega$: only a dynamically stabilized subset $\Omega_{\mathrm{eff}}(t)$ becomes realized in time.
  • Coherent Gravity: a modified metric framework that regularizes spacetime singularities.
  • Coherent Medicine: restoration of health interpreted as recovery of field-level coherence.
  • Artificial Coherent Agents: AI systems guided by coherence principles rather than pure optimization.
  • Teleportation and Identity: continuity of self maintained through coherent subjective flow.
